Chasuble (2026) — Quentin Delville

Worked as a Director of Photography

What if all it took was a pinnie to go from bully to bullied?

Alexis, a 16-year-old boy who has just transferred to a new high school, is determined to fit in with his classmates. Before long, a group of popular students catches his attention, and he becomes eager to join them. To earn their acceptance, he is willing to do whatever it takes—even if it means risking the worst.
